# ST10497750-Dean-Namagowa-WEDE5020w-POE
WEB DEVELOPMENT.
Website Goals and Objectives
 Increase website traffic through online marketing.
The goal is to boost the total number of visitors by 30% in 6 months.
 Provide easy access to information. Ensuring that all the important details the gallery, such as
events, artists, product and contact info, are easily accessible and updated.
 To measure success, we can track metrics like the number of visitors that keep coming back,
page views, and how much time spent on the site. Also we can measure the ease of access by
tracking user engagement with specific pages, like the menu or event calendar, and also gather
feedback through surveys.

Proposed Website Features and Functionality
a) Homepage: Highlights featured artists, exhibitions, and news.
b) Product page: The info about the products.The details of artists.
c) About Us: Gallery history, mission, vision, and team.
d) Events Page: Upcoming and past exhibitions with images and descriptions.
e) Contact Page: Contact form, map location, email, and phone details.
